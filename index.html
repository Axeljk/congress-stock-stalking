Welcome to Congressional Stock Stocking
  1. Search above for Congressman to check for Stock trades within the last two years.
  2. Be sure to click into the box and hit enter upon finishing Congressman name.
  3. Check the Stocks of Congressmember, with an image and some info on them.
  4. Data points:
  1. Gain/Loss: Percent change between buy/sell price. Displayed if a Representative bought/sold the same ticker in the last two years.
  2. Volume: Congress is required to report a dollar-value range for the volume of money involved in a given transaction. Can be coupled with gain/loss to estimate realized gains/loss.
  3. Forecast: Displays the highest and lowest price of a stock two months after the transaction date. Used to monitor for transactions that occurred right before a major price drop or rise.
